Types of House Cleaning

There are several different types of house cleaning. A vacation home might need a simple dusting as you spend several months out of the year not living there. If you have children or pets, then you may opt for a deep clean to take care of any hard-to-clean messes.

Basic Cleaning

This is great for people working full-time who need a little help around the home.

Deep Cleaning

Deep cleaning a house costs 300-600 on average. For packed-in dirt or messes left after parties, this can be a great option.

Declutter & Spring Cleaning

If you let your home go into disarray, a cleaning service can help you get organized. Also, a cleaning service can even help you organize home offices.

Vacant House Cleaning

The empty house is cleaned to prepare it for sale or for renters to get their deposit back. Cleaning usually includes fridge, oven, all flooring, window sills, etc.
